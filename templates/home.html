Welcome to the Weather Database!

ISAIAH OSBORNE, JOHANNES MOOLMAN, PARKER DANIELS, & MD NAHID HASAN

Two main networks, the ASOS and COOP networks, provide high quality weather observations from airport weather stations and volunteer NOAA-certified personal weather stations, respectively. While this data is important for analysis, the canonical source for historical data, the National Climate Data Center, is notoriously difficult to retrieve data from.

We hope to fix this.

All work was done as part of CSCI 4560/5560 at MTSU, Fall 2023.
